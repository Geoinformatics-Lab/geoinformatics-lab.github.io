Venue

The FOSS4G-Asia 2024 conference will be hosted at the Professor Dr. Saroj Buasri SWU Innovation Building on the Prasarnmit campus of Srinakharinwirot University (SWU) in the heart of Bangkok. Established as a center for teacher training, SWU has since evolved into a prominent hub for learning, research, and cultural exchange. Its academic offerings now span a broad spectrum, including the arts and humanities, natural and social sciences, health sciences, technology, and business administration. The Prasarnmit campus is conveniently located for attendees, with easy access to public transport via bus and metro, ensuring a smooth experience for all participants.
