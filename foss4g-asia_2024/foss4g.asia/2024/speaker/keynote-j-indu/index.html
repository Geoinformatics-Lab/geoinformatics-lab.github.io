Dr. J. Indu

Department of Civil Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Bombay
 https://drinduj.in/

J. Indu is an Associate Professor in the Remote Sensing division at the Department of Civil Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay.
Her research group works on Radar Remote Sensing and its applications in Hydrometeorology.
She spearheads India centric research activities by leading several international projects including BRICS, DST CNRS projects. She has multiple papers
published in highly reputed international journals and has been the recipient of several awards like C1973 Research Excellence award from IIT Bombay, Indo US Fellowship
for Women in STEMM (WISTEMM) etc to name a few.
