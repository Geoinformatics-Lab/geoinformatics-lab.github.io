Cancellation

If after registering you find you are unable to attend the conference you have two options:

1) Transfer your registration to a colleague; one name change is permitted and must be confirmed by email to foss4gasia@gmail.com. Requests to change a registered name before September 30th will not incur a charge. Thereafter changes will incur an administrative fee of $30.00.

2) Cancel your registration:

  • Cancellation up to 8 weeks before event will incur a 25% charge (minimum $75.00)
  • Cancellation 1 month before the event will incur a 50% charge (minimum $75.00)
  • We regret that no refunds can be made on registration cancellations after 2 November 2014 under any circumstances.

Please contact foss4g-asian at gmail.com with any questions or to modify your registration.
