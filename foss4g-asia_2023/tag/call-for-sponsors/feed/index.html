The FOSS4G-Asia Seoul team cordially invites you to consider sponsoring this exceptional conference. Your contribution will play a crucial role in ensuring the success and inclusivity of FOSS4G-Asia. The sponsorship funds will be allocated to the TGP (Travel Grant Program), benefiting attendees in need of financial assistance.


This year’s FOSS4G-Asia is particularly special for participants as it will be held as part of Seoul GeoWeek. From November 28th to December 2nd, FOSS4G-Asia, Seoul Geospatial Forum, Korea Spatial Information Society’s Fall Meeting, and UN-HABITAT’s UTC (Urban Thinkers Campus) will all be hosted at the same venue as part of Seoul GeoWeek. This provides a unique opportunity for participants to connect and network with like-minded geospatial professionals from diverse backgrounds in one location.


For details about sponsoring the FOSS4G-Asia 2023 Seoul, please see: https://foss4g.asia/2023/call-for-sponsors/

FOSS4G-Asia 2023 Seoul Team.
