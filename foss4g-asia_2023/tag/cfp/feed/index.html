I am Junyoung CHOI, co-chair of FOSS4G-ASIA 2023 Seoul.

We’d like to introduce you to the FOSS4G-Asia 2023 which will take place in Seoul at coming beautiful Autumn as an in-person event. This year’s FOSS4G-Asia will be held at Seoul hall of urbanism & architecture from 28th November to 2nd December 2023.

It is a great pleasure to invite submit your presentations, workshops, and academic abstracts on behalf of FOSS4G-ASIA 2023 co-organizer, Seoul Metropolitan Government[1], Korean Spatial Information Society[2], and OSGeo Korean Chapter[3]. Furthermore, I greatly appreciate, if you can forward this CfP to anyone who might be interested.

We have 5 different sessions such as talks, workshops, Posters(Academic Track Only), Online talks, and lightning talks(10 minutes).

Please see details of the CfP on the website

https://foss4g.asia/2023/call-for-presentations/


We are looking forward to receiving your submissions soon. Your time and support are greatly appreciated.


Yours Sincerely,
Junyoung CHOI
