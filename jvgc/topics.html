Topics in Focus


A brief list of topics that are likely to be covered by the JVGC listed below; 
  • Development and Application in Remote Sensing (RS) technology 
  • Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and Database technology 
  • Validation on RS and GIS for resource, environmental and hazard assessments 
  • Geoinformatics for landuse and urban planning and coastal zone management
  • Software and algorithm development for Geoinformatics 
  • Visualization of spatial data and 3D-GIS 
  • Communication Technology for Online Spatial Information Systems 
  • Spatial Datasets; Availability, Validation and Accessibility 
  • Perspectives for the future and new areas for joint research in Geoinformatics
